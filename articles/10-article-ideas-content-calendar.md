- [ ] Target keyword in the title (first 60 characters)

- [ ] At least one H2 contains the keyword or a variation
- [ ] 2–3 internal links to other articles on your site
- [ ] 1–2 external links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Featured image with alt text containing keyword
- [ ] Article is 800–1500 words
- [ ] Readable language — short sentences, small paragraphs
- [ ] End with a question to invite comments
