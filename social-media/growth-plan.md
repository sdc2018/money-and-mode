## Platform Strategy

### 🟥 Pinterest — Highest Priority for Traffic

Pinterest is a **search engine**, not a social network. People go there to find ideas, save them, and click through to websites. It sends the highest referral traffic of any platform for lifestyle/finance/self-improvement blogs.

**How the algorithm works:**
- Fresh pins (new images) get priority
- Consistent daily pinning matters more than volume
- Keyword-rich descriptions drive discovery
- Vertical images (2:3 ratio, 1000×1500px) perform best
- Rich Pins (once enabled) pull live article data

**Growth strategy:**

| Action | Frequency | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Pin each new article | Every publish | Freshness signal |
| Create 3–5 pin variations per article | Monthly | Different angles = more reach |
| Pin from other relevant accounts | 5–10/day | Keeps profile active |
| Add keyword-rich descriptions | Every pin | Drives search discovery |
| Join group boards in finance/lifestyle | Phase 2 | Borrow others' audiences |

**Boards to build:**
- AI Tips & Tools ✅
- Money & Financial Freedom ✅
- Life, Growth & Daily Habits ✅
- Morning Routines & Habits (add)
- Family & Connection (add — for Life & Motivation articles)
- Index Funds & Investing Simply (add)

**Realistic milestones:**
- Month 1–2: 50–100 monthly views (posting consistently)
- Month 3–6: 500–2,000 monthly views
- Month 6–12: 5,000–20,000 monthly views (if consistent + Rich Pins enabled)

### 📷 Instagram — Brand Awareness + Community

Instagram won't drive massive direct traffic (no clickable links in posts) but builds **brand recognition and trust**. People who find us on Instagram are more likely to search us on Google and subscribe.

**Content types that work for this niche:**
1. **Carousel posts** — "5 things about [topic]" with each slide being one point (highest saves/shares)
2. **Quote cards** — single powerful line from an article on a clean background
3. **Diagram posts** — our SVG diagrams work perfectly as static posts
4. **Reels** — short talking-head or text-motion videos (highest reach, hardest to produce)

**Growth strategy:**

| Action | Frequency | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Post diagram + caption per article | Every publish | Content calendar |
| Post quote card | 2–3×/week | Easy to produce, high saves |
| Use 5–8 hashtags (mix of sizes) | Every post | Discoverability |
| Reply to every comment | Within 24hrs | Algorithm boost |
| Follow accounts in niche | 10–20/day | Reciprocal follows |
| Stories: behind-the-scenes, polls, questions | 3–4×/week | Keeps existing followers engaged |

**Hashtag strategy (rotate these):**
- Broad (1M+): #financialfreedom #mindset #personalgrowth #money
- Mid (100K–1M): #financialindependence #moneyhabits #familylife #AItools
- Niche (10K–100K): #FIREmovement #indexfunds #familyconnection #passiveincomeideas

**Realistic milestones:**
- Month 1–2: 50–150 followers (mostly manual follow strategy)
- Month 3–6: 300–800 followers
- Month 6–12: 1,000–3,000 followers (if posting Reels)

### 🐦 X / Twitter — Thought Leadership + Distribution

X is best for **short-form opinions and threads** — not image-heavy content. Our Kabir Shah voice (wise, direct, experienced entrepreneur) works well here. Threads get the most reach.

**Content types that work:**
1. **Threads** — "7 things I learned from selling my company at 31" style
2. **Single punchy tweets** — one insight, one line, link to article
3. **Engagement tweets** — ask a question, spark a debate
4. **Quote tweets** — add commentary to trending finance topics

**Growth strategy:**

| Action | Frequency | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Tweet per new article | Every publish | Basic distribution |
| Write 1 original thread | Weekly | Highest reach on X |
| Engage with 5–10 finance/AI accounts | Daily | Visibility via replies |
| Retweet + comment on trending topics | As relevant | Piggyback reach |
| Pin best-performing tweet | Monthly | First impression for new visitors |

**Realistic milestones:**
- Month 1–2: 50–100 followers
- Month 3–6: 200–500 followers
- Month 6–12: 500–2,000 followers (if threads take off)
